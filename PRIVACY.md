# Privacy Policy for TrenGIF

_Last updated: June 17, 2026_

TrenGIF is a Chrome extension that searches GIFs via the GIPHY API. This policy
explains what the extension does and does not do with your information.

## What we collect

**Nothing personally identifiable.** TrenGIF does not collect, store, or sell any
personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

## How your search terms are used

When you type a search term and run a search, that term is sent over HTTPS to our
proxy server, which forwards it to the [GIPHY API](https://developers.giphy.com/)
to retrieve matching GIFs. Search terms are used only to return results and are
not associated with you or stored for any other purpose by the extension or proxy.
GIFs and the search service are provided by GIPHY; your use of them is also subject
to [GIPHY's Privacy Policy](https://support.giphy.com/hc/en-us/articles/360032872931-GIPHY-Privacy-Policy).

## Data storage

TrenGIF stores no personal data locally or remotely. The extension requests only
the minimum permission needed: network access to its own proxy host.

## Third parties

- **GIPHY** — provides GIF search and content.
- **Cloudflare** — hosts the proxy that forwards search requests.

## Changes

This policy may be updated; material changes will be reflected in this file with a
new "Last updated" date.
